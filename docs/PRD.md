# PRD — Sitios Urbanos

## 1. Product Overview

Sitios Urbanos is a **multi-tenant SaaS platform** designed to digitize and centralize the management of residential communities (propiedad horizontal).

It provides a modular system that adapts to:

- communities with security staff (portería)
- communities without security staff (self-managed)

---

## 2. Problem Statement

Residential communities suffer from:

- lack of financial transparency
- poor communication between administration and residents
- inefficient governance processes (assemblies, voting, decisions)
- fragmented tools (WhatsApp, Excel, paper, etc.)
- lack of traceability in payments and operations

---

## 3. Solution

Sitios Urbanos provides a centralized platform that:

- organizes financial operations
- enables governance and decision-making
- improves communication
- digitizes community processes
- scales with modular features

---

## 4. Target Users

### Primary Users

- Community administrators
- Residents (owners or tenants)

### Secondary Users

- Security staff (if applicable)
- Service providers (future)
- Commercial partners (future)

---

## 5. Product Dimensions

### 5.1 Core Operations
- Census 360 (units, residents, family members)
- Amenities and reservations

### 5.2 Security (Optional Wedge)
- QR access
- Parcel management
- Panic button

⚠️ Must work WITHOUT security staff

---

### 5.3 Financial System (CORE)
- Administration fees
- Extra charges
- Payment tracking
- ePayco split payments

---

### 5.4 Governance (CRITICAL MVP)
- Announcements
- PQRS
- Documents
- Basic voting

---

### 5.5 Future Ecosystem (NOT MVP)
- Marketplace
- Classifieds
- Real estate exposure
- external integrations

---

## 6. Unique Value Proposition

Sitios Urbanos differentiates by:

- combining finance + governance + operations
- working without dependency on security staff
- providing real financial traceability
- enabling scalable SaaS model

---

## 7. Business Model

Revenue streams:

1. Monthly SaaS subscription per community
2. Transaction commissions (via ePayco split)
3. Future monetization:
   - marketplace fees
   - advertising
   - premium features

---

## 8. Key Success Factors

- ease of use
- reliability
- financial accuracy
- adoption by small and medium communities
- modular scalability

---

## 9. Constraints

- must support multi-tenancy
- must enforce data isolation
- must operate under Colombian regulations
- must prioritize MVP simplicity
